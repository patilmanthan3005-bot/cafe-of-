✦ Our Story

A little corner of

Cafe of Heaven was born from a simple dream — to create a space where the chaos of everyday life melts away with the first sip of a perfectly crafted coffee. Nestled in the heart of Ahmedabad, we believe that a great cup isn't just a drink. It's a moment. A feeling. A tiny escape from the ordinary.

From bold espresso shots to vibrant fruit blends — each item on our menu is crafted with love, the finest ingredients, and an obsession with flavour that borders on the spiritual.
